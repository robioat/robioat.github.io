An Evidence-Based Interview Guide for Neurodivergent Candidates

A step-by-step method designed to help candidates get scored on their skills, not their social performance.

This guide was created to address the common mismatch between standard interview expectations and neurodivergent processing. It provides a logical framework that reduces overwhelm by focusing on structure and evidence, allowing a candidate's true abilities to be the focus.

What’s inside

  • Decode Any Job Spec: A 5-minute method to decode job specs into simple themes, turning an overwhelming document into a clear plan.
  • Learn the C.A.R. Structure: A neurodivergent-friendly alternative to the S.T.A.R. method, designed for clarity under pressure.
  • Prepare Core Evidence Stories: A system to build scorable, evidence-based answers ready for almost any competency question.
